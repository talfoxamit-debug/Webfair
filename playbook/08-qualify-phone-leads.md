# Qualifying the No-Website (Phone-Only) Leads

Your 50 no-website leads are the easiest pitch, but the list has fakes mixed in.
Here's how to tell a real, active business from noise in seconds.

## 🚩 Red flags: NOT a real prospect
1. **Keyword-stuffed name = lead-gen fake.** Real companies have *brand* names
   (Titus Fence, Morrow's Fence Company, Zepco). Fakes read like a search query:
   - "Experts Driveway Gate Repairs Boca Raton Team"
   - "Access Driveway Gate Repairs Boca Raton"
   - "Lift Master Gates Repair Mobile Service Team"
   - anything with **"Near Me"**, **"Team"**, **"Experts"**, **"Mobile Service"**
   These are created by lead-resellers to capture calls and sell them, there's no
   real business behind them to sell a website to. **Skip.**
2. **Wrong category (keyword collision).** "Pickett Fences *Family Stabilization*"
   matched "fence" but is a family-services org. Skip.
3. **Out-of-area phone.** A Boca Raton "fence company" with a 651 (Minnesota)
   number is a call center or a defunct/relocated listing. Verify before you invest time.
4. **No address + generic name.** "Fence Co", "Quick Fence Co" with no street
   address, could be a one-man side gig or a dead listing. Verify.

## ✅ Green flags: real, established
- A **distinctive brand name** + **Inc / LLC / Corp**
- A real **street address**
- A **local area code** (954 / 754 / 305 / 786 / 561)

## The 30-second test (the real qualifier)
Google **"{business name} {city}"**. A real, active business shows:
- A **Google Business Profile** (the Maps card) with **reviews and photos**
- Consistent name/address/phone
- **Recent activity**, reviews within the last year or two

A fake or dead one shows: no profile at all, or a bare listing with **0 reviews,
no photos**, or several near-identical "gate repair" listings in the same area.

> The ultimate qualifier is the call itself: a real owner picks up and talks
> fencing; a fake rings to a lead-broker or a dead line. If it's dead, mark it
> Lost in the CRM and move on, don't chase it.

## Your list, scored (`no-website-qualified.csv`)
- **🟢 CALL (33)**, established, local, addressed. Work these first, top to bottom.
- **🟡 VERIFY (11)**, 30-second Google check first (no address or out-of-area number).
- **🔴 SKIP (4)**, lead-gen fakes / wrong category. Don't waste dials.

Load them into the CRM, filter 🔥 No-website, and burn down the 🟢 list.
