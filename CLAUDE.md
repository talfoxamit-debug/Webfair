## Writing style (strict, applies to everything)

**Never use em dashes (the "—" character). Ever.** This is a standing rule from
Tal and it applies to ALL output with no exceptions: chat replies, code,
comments, commit messages, PR titles and bodies, docs, playbook files, and any
user-facing or customer-facing copy.

Use a comma, colon, period, or parentheses instead, whichever fits the sentence.
A spaced hyphen ( - ) is acceptable only where a dash is genuinely needed. Do not
substitute an en dash ("–") for an em dash either.

Keep this in mind in every single response.
