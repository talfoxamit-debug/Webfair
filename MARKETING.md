# Stackwrk: Internal Marketing Plan (Level 3)

INTERNAL: not published on the site. Companion to `STRATEGY.md` (goals/ICP)
and `PRICING-BENCHMARKS.md` (pricing). Built for a solo operator on
side-hustle hours (~10–15h/wk) with heavy automation leverage.

---

## 1. Objective (mirrors Stage A)

90 days: **5 paying clients · ≥$10k collected · ≥3 care plans.**
Funnel math to hit it: ~30 outreach/day → ~10 audit calls/mo → 2–3 closed builds/mo → ≥60% care-plan attach.

## 2. Message house (use everywhere: emails, site, calls)

**Roof (one-liner):**
> "I've built and run 4 companies. I build you the same money-making systems I run my own businesses on."

**Pillars:**
1. **Operator, not agency**: I know P&L pain, not just pixels: booking, CRM, automations, lead-gen.
2. **Fast & fixed**: live in 2–4 weeks, fixed price, no hourly meter, you own the code.
3. **Recurring peace of mind**: I don't disappear after launch (the #1 fear buyers have).

**Proof points:** live client sites (SeaTop, YatHub, Capoeira Auê) · the live audit tool ·
the interactive demos ON stackwrk.com · founding-client rates.

## 3. Channels, ranked (do them in this order)

### ① Warm operator network (highest close rate, week 1)
- List 20 business owners/partners Tal knows. One personal message each:
  *"Who do you know with an embarrassing website and money to fix it?"*
- Ask for intros, not sales. Target: 5 intros → 2 clients from network alone.

### ② Cold email engine (the core motion)
- **Sending domain:** use a separate domain for outreach (e.g. foxwrk.com, already owned)
  to protect stackwrk.com deliverability. Warm it up 2 weeks before volume.
- **List building:** Tal's own scrapers. 200 targets per beachhead vertical
  (real-estate/marine + local services). Criteria: revenue signals (reviews count,
  staff, ads running), website older than ~4 years or template-built, owner reachable.
- **Volume:** 30/day, 5 days/wk. Every email personalized with THEIR audit result.

**The killer motion, audit-led outreach:**
1. Run prospect's site through stackwrk.com/api/audit (or the UI) → grab score + top 2 failures.
2. Email references their real numbers → link to `stackwrk.com/?for=<their industry>`.
3. Landing page greets their industry, sticky CTA personalizes, audit tool lets them re-run it live.

**Sequence (3 touches, 4 days apart):**
- **T1, the audit hook:** "Ran {{their site}} through my audit tool. It scored {{score}}/100.
  Two things costing you customers: {{fail_1}} and {{fail_2}}. I recorded the fixes here: {{?for= link}}.
  Worth a 15-min call?"
- **T2, proof bump:** "Built this for a {{vertical}} business recently: {{case link}},
  {{outcome line}}. The audit gaps on your site are a 2–3 week fix at a founding-client rate. Open to a quick look?"
- **T3, breakup + urgency:** "Closing my founding-client cohort (5 spots, {{n}} left).
  Rates step up after. If now's not the time, no worries; the audit's yours either way."

**Targets to watch weekly:** open ≥50% (fix subject/deliverability if under),
reply ≥4%, call-booked ≥1.5%, close ≥25% of calls.

### ③ Vertical communities & partnerships (weeks 3+)
- Local BNI/chamber + niche FB groups/subreddits for the 2 verticals.
- Referral deal for adjacent non-competitors (bookkeepers, marketing freelancers,
  photographers): **10% of build fee** for a closed intro.

### ④ Content as proof (ongoing, low effort)
- Every launch → a 60-second before/after clip + the audit-score delta. Post to
  LinkedIn + send to the email list. The site's demos/audit tool do the talking.

### ⑤ Referral loop (built into delivery)
- At launch handoff (happiness peak): ask for 1 intro + a review, every time.
- Care-plan clients get "refer → 1 month free" standing offer.

## 4. Weekly operating cadence (side-hustle compatible)

| Day | ~Time | Action |
|---|---|---|
| Mon | 1.5h | Build/refresh list (30–50), queue week's sends, review metrics |
| Tue–Thu | 45m/day | Sends go out automated; reply handling + calls |
| Fri | 1h | Follow-ups (T2/T3), log every objection/pain in CRM, 1 content post |
| Monthly | 1h | Win/loss review → refine ICP, subjects, offer (per STRATEGY.md learning system) |

## 6. Budget (lean)

Email infra ~$30–60/mo · domain warm-up tool ~$30/mo · everything else is
already built or Tal-owned (scrapers, CRM). Total: **<$100/mo** until it proves.
No paid ads until cold email demonstrates message-market fit (cheaper signal).

## 7. What we deliberately DON'T do (yet)

Paid ads, SEO-blogging at scale, social posting schedules, cold calling, Upwork:
all defensible later; all time-sinks before message-market fit. Revisit at Stage B.
